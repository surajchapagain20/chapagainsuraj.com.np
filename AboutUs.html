ABOUT COMPANY

Travelling Peace Nepal was established in 2017, and ever since it has maintained travel management services as its core business, providing corporate, leisure and event management services. Keeping our mission and vision, “BE WHERE YOU WANT TO BE.” as a compass, through continuous investments in contemporary travel related technology and quality assurance. Peace is one of the few organizations that can offer all over the world a full spectrum of travel and tourism services with flexible and efficient solutions, as a one stop supplier. The services we provide are of high standard, IATA certified and they save time, costs and resources for our customers, whereas at the same time they enable our agent clients to compete effectively in their own source markets.

Future Plans

In the future, I plan to expand my travel agency to the other country. I will plan to earn more so that my country can take benefit from the foreign income. I would like to grow our company so that many citizens in the western country can come to Nepal at cheap prices with the smile spreading across their faces. In the following years, I would like to grow from 100 costumers to 250 costumers and I would like to grow our staff. Lastly, I would like to make my travel packages better so that more people can enjoy.
